About Ourselves

Thailand Visa &
Logistic Support Center

International Travel Corporation (ITC) is an IATA accredited agency providing a complete range of travel related services and logistics support to a number of commercial and individual clients in Dhaka and in Chittagong.

ITC was established in 1990 and within a span of three years grew to be a quality agent, which is warranted by the number of commendation certificates received from reputable airlines like British Airways, Singapore Airlines, Emirates, Thai International. ITC continues to rank among the top ten agents with the reputable airlines for the last 10 years. This achievement is primarily due to the qualified, reliable, dedicated team in ITC whose ultimate goal is customer satisfaction. ITC is managed by a highly professional team who have long years of experience of the travel trade.
